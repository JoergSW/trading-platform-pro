# Decision Log

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document records all significant architectural, technical and organizational decisions made throughout the development of Trading Platform Pro.

The goal is to preserve decision history, rationale and consequences for future developers and AI agents.

---

# Decision Principles

Every major decision should be:

- documented
- justified
- reviewable
- traceable
- version controlled

---

# Decision Record Template

Each decision should contain:

## Identifier

```
DEC-0001
```

## Title

Short descriptive title.

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Status

- Proposed
- Accepted
- Superseded
- Deprecated

## Context

Describe the problem or requirement.

## Decision

Describe the chosen solution.

## Alternatives Considered

List the evaluated alternatives.

## Consequences

Positive:

-

Negative:

-

# Maintenance

The Decision Log should be updated whenever:

- a major architectural decision is made
- a technology is replaced
- a significant design direction changes
- an accepted decision becomes obsolete

Minor implementation details should not be recorded here.
