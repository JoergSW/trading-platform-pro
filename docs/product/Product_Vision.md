# Product Vision

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision Statement

Trading Platform Pro shall provide a professional Trading Cockpit that gives active traders a unified, reliable and transparent workspace for monitoring markets, evaluating trading opportunities and managing trading workflows.

The Trading Cockpit is the primary product and the foundation for future platform capabilities.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to reduce fragmentation in professional trading workflows.

Trading Platform Pro combines market information, watchlists, trading tools, portfolio visibility, risk information and decision support in one configurable desktop workspace.

The platform shall help traders understand the current market state, identify relevant opportunities and act with greater clarity and operational confidence.

---

# Primary User Problem

Active traders often work with multiple disconnected tools, windows and data sources.

Typical problems include:

- fragmented market information
- disconnected watchlists and scanners
- limited portfolio context
- separate order and broker interfaces
- manual transfer of information between tools
- inconsistent trading workflows
- insufficient operational transparency

Trading Platform Pro shall consolidate these workflows into one Trading Cockpit.

---

# Primary Target Users

The initial product is designed primarily for:

- active private traders
- professional discretionary traders
- systematic traders using manual or semi-automated workflows

Developers and quantitative traders are secondary users where they extend or integrate platform capabilities.

AI-assisted workflows are future platform capabilities and are not the primary user group.

---

# Core Product Value

The Trading Cockpit shall provide:

- one unified trading workspace
- configurable and persistent workspaces
- consolidated market context
- fast access to relevant trading information
- transparent portfolio and risk visibility
- integrated trading workflows
- deterministic application behaviour
- reliable operational feedback

The primary product value is not the number of available features.

The primary value is the integration of trading workflows into one coherent workspace.

---

# Product Focus

The initial product focus is the Trading Cockpit.

Core capability areas are:

- Market Overview
- Watchlists
- Market Scanner
- Charts
- Portfolio Overview
- Position Management
- Order Management
- Risk Overview
- Decision Center
- Notifications
- Workspace Management

These capabilities shall be integrated into a consistent desktop application.

---

# Trading Workflow Vision

The Trading Cockpit should support the following workflow:

1. Observe the market.
2. Identify relevant instruments.
3. Evaluate market and portfolio context.
4. Analyze a trading opportunity.
5. Make a trading decision.
6. Prepare and execute an order.
7. Monitor the position and associated risk.
8. Review the trading outcome.

The user should remain within the Trading Cockpit throughout this workflow whenever practical.

---

# Workspace Vision

The Trading Cockpit shall provide configurable workspaces.

Users should be able to:

- arrange widgets
- dock and resize panels
- open multiple views
- preserve workspace state
- switch between trading workflows
- restore the previous working context

The workspace is a core product capability, not only a UI feature.

---

# Decision Support Vision

Trading Platform Pro shall support informed trading decisions.

Decision support may combine:

- market data
- watchlists
- scanner results
- portfolio context
- risk information
- trading signals
- strategy evaluations
- historical information

Future AI-assisted capabilities may provide additional insights.

The platform shall support the trader's decision process and shall not hide decision-relevant information.

---

# Product Principles

Every product capability shall:

- solve a real trading workflow problem
- integrate naturally into the Trading Cockpit
- provide clear user value
- preserve user context
- remain transparent
- behave predictably
- remain testable
- remain maintainable
- be documented

Avoid isolated features that do not contribute to a coherent trading workflow.

---

# Core Values

The product is guided by:

- Simplicity
- Reliability
- Transparency
- Clarity
- Maintainability
- Extensibility
- Performance
- Quality

---

# Non-Functional Vision

The Trading Cockpit shall provide:

- responsive desktop interaction
- deterministic runtime behaviour
- reliable state management
- comprehensive observability
- controlled error handling
- secure configuration
- automated testing
- maintainable architecture
- comprehensive documentation

Operational reliability is a product requirement.

---

# Initial Product Boundaries

The initial Trading Cockpit does not aim to provide:

- a public social trading network
- a broker replacement
- autonomous AI trading
- cloud-first multi-user operation
- high-frequency trading infrastructure
- a general-purpose financial terminal

These capabilities may only be reconsidered through explicit product and architectural decisions.

---

# Success Criteria

The Trading Cockpit is successful when users can:

- configure and restore their trading workspace
- monitor relevant market information
- manage watchlists and trading candidates
- evaluate opportunities with sufficient context
- view portfolio and risk information
- manage orders and positions
- receive clear operational feedback
- complete core trading workflows without unnecessary tool switching

Technical success additionally requires:

- stable releases
- deterministic behaviour
- maintainable architecture
- automated quality assurance
- synchronized documentation

---

# Future Platform Evolution

After the Trading Cockpit is established, the platform may evolve with:

- Portfolio Manager
- Market Scanner services
- Strategy Lab
- Backtesting
- Advanced Analytics
- Reporting Services
- Plugin Ecosystem
- AI-assisted Decision Support
- Multi-Broker Integration

Future applications shall reuse the shared Trading Platform Pro architecture and platform services.

---

# Product Evolution Principles

Every product enhancement shall:

- provide measurable user value
- support a defined trading workflow
- preserve architectural consistency
- integrate into the existing user experience
- remain maintainable
- be fully documented

---

# Product Review Checklist

Before releasing new functionality verify:

- user problem is clearly defined
- target workflow is identified
- user value is clear
- product scope is respected
- Trading Cockpit integration is defined
- architecture is preserved
- documentation is updated
- quality standards are satisfied
