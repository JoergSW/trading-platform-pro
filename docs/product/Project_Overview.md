# Project Overview

**Project:** Trading Platform Pro

**Application:** Trading Cockpit

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

Trading Platform Pro is an enterprise-grade software platform for building, operating and evolving professional trading applications.

The first application developed on this platform is the **Trading Cockpit**.

The Trading Cockpit provides an integrated workspace for market analysis, decision support, trade planning, execution, portfolio management and risk monitoring.

The platform is designed to support future applications without requiring architectural redesign.

---

# Vision

Create a modular, scalable and maintainable trading platform that combines professional software engineering with modern trading workflows.

The platform shall provide a common foundation for multiple trading applications while maintaining a consistent user experience, architecture and development model.

---

# Product Scope

The initial release focuses on the Trading Cockpit.

Core capabilities include:

- Market Monitoring
- Decision Center
- Watchlists
- Portfolio Management
- Position Monitoring
- Order Management
- Risk Management
- Reporting & Analytics
- Workspace Management
- Command Palette

# Out of Scope

The current project does not aim to provide:

- Automated strategy optimisation
- High-frequency trading
- Social trading
- Broker-specific custom solutions
- Cloud-native deployment

These capabilities may be added in future platform releases.
